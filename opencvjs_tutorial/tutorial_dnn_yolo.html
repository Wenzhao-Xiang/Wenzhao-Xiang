Usage examples

Execute in webcam:

1 $ example_dnn_object_detection --config=[PATH-TO-DARKNET]/cfg/yolo.cfg --model=[PATH-TO-DARKNET]/yolo.weights --classes=object_detection_classes_pascal_voc.txt --width=416 --height=416 --scale=0.00392 --rgb

Execute with image or video file:

1 $ example_dnn_object_detection --config=[PATH-TO-DARKNET]/cfg/yolo.cfg --model=[PATH-TO-DARKNET]/yolo.weights --classes=object_detection_classes_pascal_voc.txt --width=416 --height=416 --scale=0.00392 --input=[PATH-TO-IMAGE-OR-VIDEO-FILE] --rgb
